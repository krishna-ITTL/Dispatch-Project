## Introduction
This guide is for **Supervisors** who manage dispatch operations and oversee user activities within the Dispatch Management application.

## Minimum Requirements
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Edge, or Firefox).
- Internet connection.

## Logging In
1. Open the application URL.
2. Enter your **Username** and **Password**.
3. Click **Login**.

> *If you need credentials, contact the Digital Transformation Department.*

## Supervisor Features
- **Approve Dispatch** – Review and approve pending dispatches.
- **Reject Dispatch** – Decline a dispatch that does not meet criteria.
- **Delete Dispatch** – Remove erroneous dispatch records (restricted to supervisors).
- **View User Activity** – See a log of actions performed by regular users.
- **Generate Reports** – Export summary reports for management.

> **Note:** Password changes must be requested from the admin team.

## Handling Errors
| Symptom | Possible Cause | Suggested Action |
|---|---|---|
| Unable to approve a dispatch | Insufficient permission | Verify your supervisor role; contact admin if the role seems incorrect. |
| "Error 500" during report generation | Server issue | Wait a few minutes and retry; if persists, contact support. |
| Login fails | Wrong credentials | Confirm username/password; request reset from Digital Transformation Department. |

If issues continue, see **Contact Information**.

## Frequently Asked Questions
**Q:** How do I approve a dispatch?
**A:** Open the Dispatch List, select a pending item, and click **Approve**.

**Q:** Can I change a user’s password?
**A:** No. Password changes are handled by the admin team.

**Q:** Where can I find training materials?
**A:** This manual is the primary source; additional resources are provided by your manager.

## Confidentiality & Ethics
- Do **not** share the application link with unauthorized individuals.
- Do **not** export or distribute data outside the organization without approval.
- Follow company data protection policies; avoid any attempt to tamper with the system.
