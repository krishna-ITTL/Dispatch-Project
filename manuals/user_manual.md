## Introduction
Welcome to the **Dispatch Management** application. This guide will help you understand how to use the system for day‑to‑day tasks.

## Minimum Requirements
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Edge, or Firefox) on a computer or tablet.
- Internet connectivity.

## Logging In
1. Open your browser and navigate to the application URL provided by your organization.
2. Enter your **Username** and **Password**.
3. Click **Login**.

> *If you do not have credentials, contact the Digital Transformation Department.*

## Features & Options
- **Create Dispatch** – Record a new dispatch order.
- **View Dispatch List** – See all current and past dispatches.
- **Search** – Find a dispatch by reference number or date.
- **Export** – Download dispatch data as Excel.
- **Profile Settings** – Update personal details (except password).

> **Note:** Changing your password requires admin assistance (see Contact Information).

## Handling Errors
| Symptom | Possible Cause | Suggested Action |
|---|---|---|
| Page does not load | Network issue | Refresh the page or check your connection. |
| "Error 500" message | Server issue | Wait a few minutes and retry. |
| Login fails | Wrong credentials | Verify username/password; contact admin if needed. |

If the problem persists, see the **Contact Information** section.

## Frequently Asked Questions
**Q:** How do I reset my password?
**A:** Contact the Digital Transformation Department – they will reset it for you.

**Q:** Can I delete a dispatch record?
**A:** Only users with Supervisor role can delete records.

**Q:** Where can I find help documentation?
**A:** This manual is the primary reference. Additional help is available via the **Help** button in the app.

## Confidentiality & Ethics
- Do **not** share the application link with unauthorized persons.
- Do **not** copy, redistribute, or post screenshots publicly.
- Treat all dispatch data as confidential; do not attempt to hack, scrape, or manipulate the system.
- Follow your organization’s data protection policies.
